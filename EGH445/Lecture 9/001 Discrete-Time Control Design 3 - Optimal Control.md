# Discrete-Time Control Design 3: Optimal Control

## Lecture Overview

This lecture covers optimal control as a systematic alternative to pole placement, starting with a review of discrete-time systems and the limitations of pole placement, then introducing the Discrete Linear Quadratic Regulator (DLQR) and Model Predictive Control (MPC).
