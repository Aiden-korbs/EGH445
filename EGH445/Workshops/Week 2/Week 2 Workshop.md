## Topic Overview: 
System Response and Stability This workshop focuses on solving state-space equations to understand system behavior over time and analysing the internal and external stability of these systems .

## Concepts
* **System Response:** * [[System Response and Solutions]]: Methods for finding numerical and analytical solutions to state equations .
* **Stability:**
	* [[Stability Analysis in State-Space]]: Distinctions between BIBO (external) and Internal stability .
	* [[Lyapunov Stability]]: Using energy functions to determine the stability of an equilibrium point .
* **Applied Examples:**
	* [[Week 2 Workshop Applications]]: Applying response and stability analysis to the 6DOF B747 Aircraft and Hodgkin-Huxley Neuron models .
