# Aircraft State and Motion Variables

## Position Coordinates
- $x$, $y$, $z$ are **position coordinates**.

## Velocity Coordinates
- $u$, $v$, $w$ are **velocity coordinates**.

## Angular Rates
- $p$ is **roll rate**.
- $q$ is **pitch rate**.
- $r$ is **yaw rate**.

## Angles
- $\phi$ is **roll angle**.
- $\theta$ is **pitch angle**.
- $\psi$ is **yaw angle**.

## Aerodynamic Angles
- $\beta$ is **side-slip angle**.
- $\alpha$ is **angle of attack**.

## Why This Note Matters
- These variables appear across the workshop aircraft example.
- Keeping the notation separate makes the modelling notes easier to read.
