# Biological Model Running Example

## Role in the Workshop
- The second running example is a **biological model**.
- It appears after the aircraft example to show that control ideas apply beyond aerospace systems.

## What the Slides Show
- A neuron-like biological illustration with an electrical circuit analogue.
- Differential equations involving membrane voltage and gating variables.

## Key Point
- The workshop explicitly says these equations are included to **motivate the examples**.
- You are **not required** to memorise or fully understand the detailed equations here for the workshop.

## Conceptual Takeaway
- Control modelling ideas can be applied to:
  - biological systems
  - circuit analogues
  - nonlinear state dynamics
