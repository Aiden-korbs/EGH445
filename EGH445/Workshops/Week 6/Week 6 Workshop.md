## Focus
- This workshop ties together the lecture material on:
  - **controllability**
  - **observability**
  - **direct discrete-time design**
  - **regulation with zero and non-zero reference**
- The main applied example is a **$6$DOF aircraft model** used to show how modelling, design, and evaluation connect.
