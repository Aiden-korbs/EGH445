# Biological Model Workshop Reference

## Role in the Workshop
- The biological model is presented as a secondary workshop example.
- It is not the main focus of the Week 8 control design discussion.

## Key Note
- The workshop explicitly indicates that the detailed equations on the final pages are mainly to motivate later examples.
- The important takeaway is the **high-level connection** between modelling and control, not memorising every equation.

## Shown Content
- A neuron-like biological structure diagram
- A circuit-style representation
- Nonlinear differential equations involving membrane voltage and gating variables

## Variables Mentioned
- Membrane voltage:
$$
V_m
$$
- Gating variables:
$$
n,\; m,\; h
$$

## Practical Takeaway
- This example signals that the same modelling-and-control mindset applies beyond aerospace systems.
