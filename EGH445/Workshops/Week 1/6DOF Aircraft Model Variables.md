## Aircraft Coordinate and State Definitions
When modeling the 6 Degrees of Freedom (6DOF) dynamics of an aircraft, specific variables are assigned to track positions, velocities, angles, and rotational rates . 

**Position & Velocity Coordinates:**
* **Position:** Defined by the coordinates $x$, $y$, and $z$ .
* **Linear Velocity:** Defined by the coordinates $u$, $v$, and $w$ .

**Angles & Rotational Rates:**
* **Roll:** The roll angle is denoted by $\phi$, and the roll rate is $p$ .
* **Pitch:** The pitch angle is denoted by $\theta$, and the pitch rate is $q$ .
* **Yaw:** The yaw angle is denoted by $\psi$, and the yaw rate is $r$ .
* **Aerodynamic Angles:** The side-slip angle is $\beta$, and the angle of attack is $\alpha$ .

**Control Inputs (Actuators):**
* **Aileron:** Denoted as $\delta_a$ .
* **Rudder:** Denoted as $\delta_r$ .
* **Elevators:** Denoted as $\delta_e$ .
