## Topic Overview: 
System Modelling Workshop This workshop explores practical applications of modern control theory by modelling complex, multi-variable systems . 

## Concepts
* **Running Example 1: 6DOF Aircraft Model**
	* [[6DOF Aircraft Model Variables]]: Definitions of states, velocities, and control inputs .
	* [[6DOF Aircraft Nonlinear Equations]]: The foundational forces, moments, and kinematic equations .
	* [[6DOF Aircraft Linearised Dynamics]]: The decoupled longitudinal and lateral state-space models .
* **Running Example 2: Biological Model**
	* [[Hodgkin-Huxley Neuron Model]]: The electrical circuit equivalent for a neuron membrane .
	* [[Hodgkin-Huxley Activation Gates]]: The nonlinear state-space equations governing ionic flow .
